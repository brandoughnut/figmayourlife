Subaru Natsuki is a character from the anime Re:Zero. He originally lived in Japan, but was mysteriously transported into a new world that extremely different from Earth.

Subaru tends to not read the situations he encounters, ultimately leading him to make irrational decisions, he is loyal to those he holds dear, willing to help them, even if that means taking the thorny path. Beneath his eccentric demeanor, however, Subaru is actually a very insecure person suffering from a severe inferiority complex.

When Subaru was transported, he gained a power called Return by Death. This power made it so that whenever Subaru would die, he would return back to a point in time and still keep his memories. In the events of the story, some things do not go his way, so he relies on this power to achieve what his goals.
